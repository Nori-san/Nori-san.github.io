Location

Nestled within the serene landscapes of the Gaspé peninsula, Lions Academy stands as a beacon of progressive education and holistic development. 1501, boulevard Forillon, Gaspé QC, G4X 6M1

Established in 1898 by visionary educator Dr. Joseph Lions, Lions Academy emerged during a transformative era in educational philosophy. Dr. Lions believed that traditional rote learning stifled the potential of students, and he sought to create an institution that would foster a harmonious blend of academic rigour and introspection.

The academy's early years were marked by its unique approach to curriculum, integrating arts, nature, exploration, and community engagement into traditional subjects. This holistic approach drew attention from educators worldwide, leading to the first International Holistic Education Symposium hosted at Lions Academy in 1925.

Throughout the decades, Lions Academy continued to evolve. The 1960s saw the creation of the Harmony Gardens, where students and faculty collaborated to cultivate a sustainable organic garden, reinforcing the school's commitment to environmental stewardship. The 1980s brought the establishment of the "Global Awareness Program," allowing students to engage with peers from various countries through pen-pal initiatives and cultural exchange programs.

In the 21st century, Lions Academy embraced technology while maintaining its core values. The "Virtual Classroom Project" launched in 2005 enabled students to interact with experts and access resources from around the world, broadening their horizons beyond the classroom walls. Additionally, the "Leadership for Social Change" initiative empowered students to tackle pressing global issues, spawning numerous community-driven projects.

Today, Lions Academy continues to be at the forefront of innovative education. Its tree-lined pathways, state-of-the-art sustainable campus, and diverse student body reflect its dedication to fostering well-rounded individuals who are not only academically adept but also compassionate global citizens. As it celebrates its 125th anniversary, Lions Academy remains steadfast in its commitment to shaping future generations with the wisdom of the past and the aspirations of the future.
